Mines and Minerals

SangEES supports the mining and mineral industries through targeted engineering, with a particular focus on rare earth metals — the cornerstone of future modern engineering and energy solutions.

Expertise Includes:

  • Mineral Processing: crushing, grinding, classification, dewatering
  • Particle Characterization: PSD, shape, density, moisture
  • Process Modeling: DEM & CFD of bulk solids
  • Material Handling: hopper/conveyor design, dust control

Applications:

  • Cement, metal ores, rare earths, non-metallic minerals

Sustainability:

  • Energy/water saving technologies
  • Dry beneficiation, tailings recovery

Why SangEES?

  • Deep mineral + material science integration
  • Field-ready solutions
  • Scalable engineering workflows
  • Bridging small and large-scale: our AI prototyping can provide micro-level details as well as a holistic view of the systems
