Sun-Dependent Energy Solutions

SangEES designs sun-powered systems for clean, renewable energy deployment.

Energy Types:

  • Solar: PV, solar thermal, storage systems, concentrated solar power (CSP), solar-powered water pumping, solar street lighting, off-grid solar kits.
  • Wind: Site analysis, turbine integration, hybrid grids, micro-wind for remote locations, wind resource measurement (LiDAR, met masts), maintenance and performance optimization.
  • Hydro: Small hydro & run-of-river designs, micro-hydro for rural electrification, water turbine retrofitting, grid and off-grid hydro integration.
  • Waves & Tidal: Coastal energy feasibility, tidal stream turbine deployment, wave energy converter integration, coastal protection with energy capture systems.

Services:

  • Site/resource analysis
  • Feasibility and simulation (PV*Sol, HOMER)
  • Environmental review and impact assessment
  • Grid connection studies and utility liaison
  • Energy storage design and optimization (batteries, hydrogen, pumped hydro)
  • Hybrid system design (mixing solar, wind, diesel, and storage)
  • Load forecasting and demand analysis
  • Project Financing Advisory (CAPEX, OPEX, Payback, ROI)
  • Permitting and Regulatory Compliance Support
  • Detailed Engineering Design (Civil, Mechanical, Electrical)
  • Procurement and Supplier Selection
  • Construction Supervision and Commissioning
  • Performance Monitoring and O&M Services
  • System Upgrades and Retrofits for Efficiency Improvement
  • Community Training and Capacity Building
